Meet the team

ABOUT US

We are a group of UI/UX/Product professionals who are on a mission to empower university students across Ghana with the skills they need to create products that people use and love, as well as introducing them to the tools they need in order to succeed. We are doing this by organising free 1 day workshops on university campuses across Ghana.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Empowerment

We empower university students with the best product design tools and skills. This is to give them the needed advantage to compete in the job market.

Community building

UX on campus has created a community of designers and design lovers to come together to learn from each other and make connections because the future is community.

Mobilisation

We bring together UX professionals from across the country to give talks and mentor our participants in areas of UI/UX design and related disciplines.
